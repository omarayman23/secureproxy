Frequently Asked Questions

How does SecureProxy protect my privacy?

SecureProxy routes your internet traffic through our secure proxy servers, masking your real IP address and encrypting all data transfers. This prevents websites, ISPs, and other third parties from tracking your online activities or determining your real location.

Is SecureProxy free to use?

Yes, SecureProxy offers a free tier with unlimited browsing through our basic proxy servers. We also offer premium plans with faster speeds, more server locations, and additional security features.

Can websites still track me?

With SecureProxy enabled, websites cannot see your real IP address or location. However, if you log into personal accounts or provide identifying information, websites may still associate that activity with your account. For maximum anonymity, avoid logging into personal accounts while using the proxy.

Does SecureProxy slow down my internet?

There may be a slight reduction in speed due to the encryption and routing through our proxy servers. However, we optimize our network for performance and have servers worldwide to minimize latency. Most users experience minimal speed impact during normal browsing.

Which websites can I access?

You can access virtually any website through SecureProxy. Our service works with all standard web protocols and is compatible with popular websites including search engines, social media platforms, streaming services, and e-commerce sites.

Is my data stored or logged?

No, we maintain a strict no-logs policy. We do not store your browsing history, IP addresses, or any personal information. All session data is automatically deleted when you close your browser or disconnect from our service.

Troubleshooting

Common Issues & Solutions

Website not loading

If a website doesn't load through the proxy, try these solutions:

  • Check if the URL is correctly formatted (include https://)
  • Try selecting a different proxy server in settings
  • Clear your browser cache and cookies
  • Disable any conflicting browser extensions

Slow connection speeds

If you experience slow browsing speeds:

  • Choose a proxy server closer to your location
  • Check your internet connection without the proxy
  • Try browsing during off-peak hours
  • Consider upgrading to our premium service for faster speeds

Connection errors

If you can't connect to the proxy service:

  • Check if your firewall is blocking the connection
  • Try refreshing the page and attempting to connect again
  • Verify that your internet connection is working
  • Contact our support team if issues persist
